How To Play
  • Player 1 starts by rolling the dice.
  • Their roll is added to their current score
  • Each player can roll as many times as they like.
  • The current player can click "Hold" to add their current score to their total score and pass the turn to the other player.
  • If a player rolls a 1, their current score becomes 0 and it becomes the other player's turn.
  • If two sixes in a row are rolled, the current player's total score becomes 0 and it becomes the other player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins!
